About Blood Request Tracking

Once you submit a blood request, you will receive a unique Request ID. Use this ID to track the status of your request at any time.

Status Meanings:

  • Pending - Your request is being processed and we're looking for matching donors
  • Approved - Donors have been matched to your request
  • Completed - The required units of blood have been collected
  • Rejected - Your request could not be fulfilled
